$f(g(x))=(fog)(x)$
*f of g of x*
$\frac{d}{dx}(f(g(x)))=f^1(g(x))*g^1(x)$
$f^1(g(x))$ - Derivative of outer Function
$g^1(x)$ - Derivative of inner Function
#### Example
$h(x)=sin(x^2)$ find $h^1(x)$
$f(x) = sin(x)$ - $g(x)=x^2$
$f^1(x)=cos(x)$ - $g^1(x)=2x$

$h(x)=f(g(x))$
$h^1(x)=f^1(g(x))*g^1(x)$
$h^1(x)=cos(x^2)*2x$
$h^1(x)=2xcos(x^2)$
#### Example

$f^1(x)=e^x$ | $g^1(x)=-2$

$h(x)=f(g(x))$
